Privacy Policy for PoE Reputation Advisor

Effective Date: August 13, 2025

Overview

This browser extension is designed to display and manage trader reputation on the official Path of Exile trade website. We respect your privacy and do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your real name, email address, or physical address).

Data We Access

  • Publicly available trader information (such as in-game account names or IDs) displayed on the Path of Exile trade website.
  • This data is retrieved directly from the website you are visiting and is not linked to any personal identifiers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, payment details, or any other private information.
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of the Path of Exile trade website.

Data Storage and Processing

Trader ratings and related data may be stored on our backend server for the sole purpose of providing the reputation feature. No sensitive or personal data is stored.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not share collected data with third-party advertisers or analytics providers.
